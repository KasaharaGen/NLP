Q: Create and distribute printers Is there a way to create a printer on a machine. Then bundle up the Printer bundle and create an installer out of it for multiple users at a location. To avoid having to explain to each user how to install a network printer?
This is one of those enterprise issues.
(10.6 specifically... at least for the next month)

A: If your Macs are part of the OS X Server's OD, then you can use Workgroup Manager to assign printers to either Groups of users or Groups of Machines.
If you want to manage the drivers and the detailed printer settings, this link might help.
