Q: Can I use PayPal for non-US iTunes Store payments? Can I use PayPal for non-US iTunes Store payments?
I've heard US users can do so, but my PayPal and iTunes account are non-US

A: Payment methods vary greatly among the many iTunes stores around the world and are often fit to the local market. In Germany, for example, it's possible to pay via direct-debit, because this is a very common way to pay there. There are iTunes stores (additionally to the US one) which accept PayPal - UK comes to mind. In your case I would check my iTunes account to see whether it is possible to pay via PayPal.

A: One way to pay in foreign stores is to buy gift vouchers (i.e. on ebay) and open a second account with a fake address and use these vouchers.
