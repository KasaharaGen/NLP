Q: Outlook-iPhone and private email How can I send private email from my iphone to an Outlook user so his delegates cannot see the email?

A: You can setup the permissions of the Delegate so they can not see private items. Here's a KB article about granting permission to them:
http://support.microsoft.com/kb/249324
The trick is now finding a way to get your iPhone to add the "Private" flag to your message. I don't have an iPhone so I can't help you there. Just edit the properties of your message and see what you can change.
